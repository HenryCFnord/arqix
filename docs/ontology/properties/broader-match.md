## broader-match

The mapped external concept is broader than the document's subject (ADR-0022).
A refinement of maps-to; the inverse perspective of narrower-match.
The target is the external concept's IRI; domain and range stay undeclared, so any document may map and the external target stays outside the corpus graph.
