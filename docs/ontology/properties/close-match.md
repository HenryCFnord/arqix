## close-match

The mapped external concept and the document's subject are close enough to substitute in many contexts, but not interchangeable (ADR-0022).
A refinement of maps-to.
The target is the external concept's IRI; domain and range stay undeclared, so any document may map and the external target stays outside the corpus graph.
