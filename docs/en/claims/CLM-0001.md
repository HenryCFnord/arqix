## RFC 2119 Keyword Subset Extraction

### Analysis

The claim: the keyword rules of the requirement style family (the SHALL/SHOULD/MAY subset behind EARS-003 and EARS-004) restate the requirement-level key words of RFC 2119.
The analyzed representation is the captured local copy `sources/rfc2119.txt`, pinned by the digest above — the same bytes the source record SRC-0002 verifies (SRC-006).
The extraction narrowed the RFC's vocabulary to the arqix subset: MUST/REQUIRED/OPTIONAL and the RECOMMENDED forms are deliberately excluded (EARS-003 reports them), which is an authoring decision on top of the source, not part of it.
The record is unreviewed; a reviewer confirms or rejects by setting the review verdict and their name.
